Data Privacy Notice

In compliance with the Philippines Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173), Upper Labay High School ensures that all student data processed by this SARDO Risk System is handled with the highest level of security. This system operates on a "Cloud-Native" architecture:

Predictive Analytics

Risk scores are calculated using historical attendance data only. No sensitive personal information beyond what is already stored is collected.

Role-Based Access

Access is strictly limited to Admin, School Head, Guidance Counselor, and Teachers with attendance privileges.

Intervention Focused

Risk scores are for internal use only to identify students who may need additional support and intervention.
