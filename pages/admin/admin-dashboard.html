Data Privacy Notice

In compliance with the Philippines Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173), Upper Labay High School ensures that all student data processed by this Attendance System is handled with the highest level of security. This system operates on a "Secured Cloud" architecture:

Encrypted Storage

Data is stored in an encrypted Supabase database. Access is strictly limited to authorized school personnel via encrypted authentication.

Audit Trails

Every scan and access request is logged, providing a transparent audit trail that prevents unauthorized data tampering or leaks.

Rights Protection

Collected data is used strictly for official DepEd reporting. Students and parents retain all rights to access, correct, or request deletion of their data.
