Privacy Policy

Upper Labay High School

1. Overview

In compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173), Upper Labay High School (ULHS) is committed to protecting the personal information of its students and parents. This policy explains how we collect and process data for the Attendance Alert System via Facebook Messenger.

2. Data Collection

To provide automated attendance alerts, we collect the following:

  • Student Information: Name and Learner Reference Number (LRN).
  • Parent Information: Facebook Page Scoped ID (PSID) upon successful linking.
  • Attendance Logs: Time of arrival and departure from the school campus.

3. Purpose of Processing

The data is processed solely for the purpose of notifying parents/guardians about their child's school attendance in real-time. We do not use this data for marketing, profiling, or third-party sharing.

4. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored securely in Cloud Database with strict Row Level Security (RLS) policies. Access is restricted to authorized school personnel only.

5. Data Deletion & User Rights

In compliance with the DPA, users have the right to have their data deleted from our system. To request data deletion:

Method 1 (Instant): Send the message UNLINK [LRN] to our Messenger bot. This immediately removes your Facebook ID from the student record.

Method 2 (Manual): Contact the ULHS ICT Department or message our official Facebook Page with a formal request to purge your notification data.

6. Contact Us

For questions regarding your data privacy, please contact our Data Protection Officer at the Upper Labay High School ICT Department or visit our official Facebook Page.
